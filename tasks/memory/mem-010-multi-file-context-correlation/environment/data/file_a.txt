Knowledge is the foundation upon which all great achievements are built. Throughout
history, civilizations have risen and fallen based on their ability to accumulate,
preserve, and transmit understanding from one generation to the next. The ancient
Library of Alexandria stood as a monument to this truth, housing scrolls that
contained the collective wisdom of the Mediterranean world. When it was lost,
centuries of learning disappeared with it, setting back human progress in ways
we can barely comprehend today. Modern institutions of learning carry forward
this same essential mission, ensuring that what we discover today will illuminate
the path for those who come after us.
