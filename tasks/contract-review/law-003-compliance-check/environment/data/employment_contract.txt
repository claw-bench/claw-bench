EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This agreement is entered into between Northbridge Analytics Ltd. (the "Employer") and the undersigned individual (the "Employee"). The Employee accepts the conditions stated herein and acknowledges that this document is governed by the laws of the jurisdiction in which the Employer operates.

1. Compensation. The Employee shall receive a minimum wage as prescribed by law, with the base salary reviewed annually.

2. Working Hours. Working hours shall be 40 hours per week. Any overtime worked beyond the standard schedule shall be authorized in advance.

3. Overtime Compensation. Overtime worked beyond standard hours will be compensated at 1.5 times the normal hourly rate.

4. Salary Payment. Salary will be paid monthly on the last working day of each calendar month via direct bank transfer.

5. Paid Leave. The Employee is entitled to 20 days of paid leave annually, accrued on a pro-rata basis during the first year of service.

6. Holiday Entitlement. The Employee is entitled to public holiday days off with full pay in accordance with the local calendar.

7. Sick Leave. Sick leave of up to 10 days per year is granted upon presentation of appropriate medical documentation.

8. Probation Period. The probation period shall be 3 months from the start date, during which performance will be assessed.

9. Job Description. The Employee shall perform the duties outlined in the attached job description and any reasonable related tasks.

10. Termination Notice. A termination notice period of 30 days is required from either party, to be submitted in writing.

11. Confidentiality. The Employee must maintain strict confidentiality of all proprietary company information during and after employment.

12. Intellectual Property. All intellectual property created by the Employee in the course of employment belongs to the Employer.

13. Data Protection. The Employee must comply with all applicable data protection laws when handling personal information.

14. Workplace Safety. The Employer will ensure a safe working environment and uphold all safety obligations under applicable regulations.

15. Equal Opportunity. The company offers equal opportunity employment and prohibits discrimination of any kind.

16. Dispute Resolution. Disputes arising under this agreement will be resolved through good-faith negotiation and, if unresolved, binding arbitration.

17. Child Labor. The employment of persons under 18 years of age is strictly prohibited.

SIGNED by both parties as of the effective date written above.
